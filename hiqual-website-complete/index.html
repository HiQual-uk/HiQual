About Us

Built on one rule: safety is non-negotiable.

HiQual United Kingdom Institute is a professional Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) training provider based in Regent's Park, Central London. We train workers, supervisors and safety officers across construction, oil & gas, manufacturing and general industry — equipping them with practical, internationally recognised safety skills.

Every certificate we issue is entered into our verification system, so employers anywhere in the world can confirm it's genuine in seconds.

Our Vision

To be a globally trusted name in HSE training, recognised for producing safety-conscious professionals who protect lives at work.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, practical and internationally aligned HSE training — backed by transparent, verifiable certification for every learner.
